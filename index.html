Features of QRent

As QRent, we basically hope to combine QR technologies with real estate. While doing this, we aim to both provide convenience to the user and reduce the responsibility of the host. As QRent, aiming to bring a very minimal solution to hotels and short-term house rentals, especially in today's ecosystem, we aim to bring together the tenant and the landlord in the most appropriate way for both parties. For this, we aim to use a lock scheme linked to an application. In this way, we are planning to activate a system that will work 24/7 in the house rental business, which requires face-to-face meetings and is tiring for both parties, that will waste almost no time and reduce human interaction as much as possible.

QR Technologies

Our application offers the user the opportunity to rent the house they want to use very easily, thanks to the QR. It achieves this by placing a customized QR code on the door of each rentable house. Thus, the user can easily rent that house by simply scanning the QR code.

Remote Lock System

The user has the opportunity to open the door of the rented house with the QR code, whenever he wants, with his phone. In this way, neither a key delivery nor a key is needed! At the same time, thanks to the control of the door opening status for emergencies, it becomes easier to intervene if necessary, and user safety is brought to the highest level.

Management of House

The conveniences provided by our application do not end with the lessor, but at the same time, it provides many great conveniences to the tenant. The most important of these is to reduce the opportunity losses that the Tenant may encounter to zero. At the same time, QRent, which brings a standard to renting a house, helps to establish a mutual trust between the tenant and the lessor.
